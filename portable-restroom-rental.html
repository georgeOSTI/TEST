FAQ

Portable Restroom Rental Questions

How quickly can a portable restroom be delivered?

In most cases we can schedule delivery within a few business days across the Coachella Valley. Call (760) 701-0814 to check current availability for your dates.

Do you provide servicing (pump-out and cleaning) during the rental?

Yes. A service schedule is arranged when you book, and our own trucks handle pumping and cleaning — see our septic tank pumping service.

Is an ADA-accessible unit required for my job site?

Many construction and public-facing sites are required to provide ADA-accessible restrooms. Our ADA unit ($380/mo) includes support bars and extra interior space to meet that need — ask us if you're unsure what your site requires.

Can I rent for a single day, or only monthly?

Our standard published rates are monthly. For single-day events, weddings, or festivals, call us directly and we'll put together short-term pricing for your date.

Need restrooms for your next job or event?

Tell us your site details and headcount — we'll recommend the right units and get you a quote same day.
